How to Play

Use the arrow buttons or keys to move the vehicle, carrying objects to designated position.

  • ⬆️ button or Up Arrow: Move forward
  • ⬅️ or Left Arrow: Turn left
  • ➡️ or Right Arrow: Turn right
  • ⏹ or Spacebar: Pick up or drop off objects
  • 🆗 or Return: Finish the level at the starting position
Complete the tasks and Go to starting position to finish the level!
